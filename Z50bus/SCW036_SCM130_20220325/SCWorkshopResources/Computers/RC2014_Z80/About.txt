The RC2014 range of computer kits comes in two formats:
1/ Single board computer (RC2014 Mini)
2/ Modular computer consisting of backplane and various plug in boards

Kits range from 8k ROM and 32k RAM, to 64k ROM and 64k RAM.

There is also a module available with 512k ROM and 512k RAM.

Supplied software ranges from BASIC only, to CP/M.
